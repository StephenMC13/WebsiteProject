Who I am

I am Stephen McNern. I am photographer and computing student from County Donegal. My photography is largely based around the Donegal area hence the name of the website.
I enjoy hiking and motorcycle touring alongside photography. I try to combine my hobbies as often as I can.

What I use

For digital photography I use prefer my Sony A7 III and for film photography I use a Minolta XG-7.
I tend to favour fixed lenses in the 28mm-50mm range and a 70mm-200mm for photos that work best with some background compression.
